About Me

  A near two decades of existence with untold numbers of knowledge, wisdom, and experience garnered. I’ve exhausted every part of my resources to reach where I am now. I, Tenchie M. Adolfo a proud Bachelor of Science in Computer Science student aspiring to make change within the world.

  The life I have led has been a long, rough, and bumpy road but I must also state that I am quite fortunate than the rest. I was raised by my family with a mix of sweet, tough, and tender love. We may not have come from a family that has a vast amount of wealth, but we were wealthy with our well-equipped mind and cared for each other even in toughest times.

  An old soul is who I am, mind of a man trapped in the physical body of a boy. I’ve always been curious of all the things around me for the questions on “how things work or what it is?” like how Albert Einstein had focused on wonders of the cosmos and discovered his Theory of Relativity, must be one of the reasons why many recognize my matured mind at the age lower than half of the people existing within this world, though it may also because I have gone through hardships even in my childhood which I all clearly remember as if it had happened yesterday. I’ve never dragged anything with me as negative, a mind that is examined leads to a meaningful life just like how the Greek philosophers has taught us throughout the decades. A stoic mind that follows reasoning and logic with a bit of support of faith and belief has allowed me to produce achievements through the length of my existence up until the present. Going back in time to the age of four to seven I could play around with hardware parts of a omputer and navigate the internet to its early days, I can even play GTA San Andreas, World of Warcraft, and those alike at that age. The little boy being “techy” was also athletic even at the time. I don’t know as well, but I pick up things fast and when I understand it, it stays with me.

  The child Chie wasn’t always at the top of all things, but he is guaranteed to always excel at all things. The basic school was easy for me, I never tried so hard yet I’m always on the stage at the end of the year receiving awards from various school meets and competitions I was sent to. I always favored science yet struggled a little with mathematics even with that as a hindrance I’m able to execute every task I was given. Throughout every grade level, I compete from athletic points to academically inclined challenges. I even once tried editorial cartooning as I loved to draw when I was a kid, though there were people that were better than me, but I wasn’t left behind. I also competed for the defense of a Science Investigatory Project that was conducted by my adviser with her colleagues. There were three of us that were chosen to defend the study but ended up me being the only one answering questions from the panel during the defense.

  The teenager Chie made tons of mistakes as a high school student, a lover, and a person but he made sure to always learn from it and he did. He ran for student government organizations, competed for newscasting, danced in school events, played guitar for the school, and a whole lot more. This was also the time he fell in love with cycling. I’ve reached places that were far from the technology, explored the nature to its finest, felt the breeze of the wind as I breakthrough every blow of it, endured the heavy heat of the sun in pavement roads, swam through bodies of water in front of me, took risks in every journey I’ve gone. He ran the roads of the city and embraced everything in it. Played volleyball, tried basketball and shot through the ring of the opposing team and multiple types of sports. He learned everything and was good at it, but the greatest part of it all was, He fell in love and got his heart broken. However, it was all a lovely experience but the best of it all, he always learned from every part of it.

  Lastly, allow me to introduce myself again, I, Tenchie M. Adolfo the Architect, Logician, Leader, Defender, Moribus, Entrepreneur, Athlete, and a Lover who always loved to gain wisdom and knowledge. A wandering soul in this vast universe where I am only considered as dust and a little part of the whole will always take his stand and learn from everything. The one who always craves for more a never-ending greed of curiosity with the existence of all. The one who opposes the natural and believes that death isn’t the end of it all. The one who stands still in the face of an insurmountable challenge. A tranquil soul and mind that remains neutral in state of all chaos. An epistemophile in every existing information that was cultivated in the life of Earth. The one who asks questions that were never thought of, the mind that always seeks for an answer. The one that takes apart every material object in front of him and explores the parts of it to understand how it all works. The one who takes the word of philosophers and physicists as a part of his own values and principles. The man who follows his word and bound by it. The man that believes that we only die if we are forgotten. So, I will make this name sound and reach the heavens so we will never be forgotten.
