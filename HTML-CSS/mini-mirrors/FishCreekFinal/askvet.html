Contact us if you have a question that you would like answered here.

Our dog, Sparky, likes to eat whatever kids are snacking on. Is it OK for the dog to eat chocolate?
Chocolate is toxic to dogs. Please do not feed your dog with chocolate. Try playing a game with you children – when you feed them people treats, they can feed Sparky dog treats.
